Challenge Statement
Develop an integrated Wealth Wellness Hub that unifies traditional and digital assets while embedding financial wellness analytics to deliver a complete, actionable view of an investor’s total financial health. 
Context 
Investors increasingly manage wealth across fragmented ecosystems — from bank deposits and investment portfolios to private assets and digital wallets. This fragmentation obscures their true financial picture and limits their ability to make informed, holistic decisions. 
Emerging technologies in data analytics, behavioral finance, and open finance APIs now make it possible to consolidate and transform this data into actionable financial intelligence. 
A Wealth Wellness Hub would serve as a secure, integrated Wealth Wallet that aggregates all assets and acts as a real-time financial health monitor — empowering clients and advisers to assess diversification, identify opportunities, and enhance long-term financial resilience. 
Expected Features
Unify and secure data from existing portfolios, private holdings, and digital assets into a single Wealth Wallet. 
Analyze and assess financial wellness using metrics such as diversification, liquidity, and behavioral resilience. 
Visualize overall wealth composition and track health indicators over time. 
Recommend personalized, data-driven actions and scenario-based insights for clients and advisers.
Deliver an intuitive, interactive digital dashboard that enhances clarity, trust, and proactive engagement.
